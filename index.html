1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Viori Studio handles information in Swibrary. Swibrary is intended for reading and organizing diary-book files on your device.

Swibrary does not require an account, does not show advertising, and does not use analytics or tracking SDKs in the current version.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version, Swibrary does not collect, transmit, sell, or share the following information:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address
  • Diary text, diary-book content, photos, highlights, bookmarks, or notes
  • Precise location data
  • Device identifiers for advertising or tracking
  • Usage analytics or crash analytics sent to Viori Studio

3. Local App Data

Swibrary may store app data locally on your device so the app can function properly. This may include imported diary-book files, bookshelf organization, reading position, bookmarks, highlights, display preferences, and other reader settings.

This data is stored on your device and is not uploaded to Viori Studio servers by Swibrary.

4. File Access

Swibrary may ask you to choose files from your device, such as diary-book files created by Swiv or other supported reading files. File access is used only to open, import, display, organize, or export files that you select.

Swibrary does not scan your entire device storage, and selected files are not uploaded to Viori Studio servers.

5. Data Sharing

Viori Studio does not sell your personal data. In the current version, Swibrary does not share your diary-book content or reading data with third parties.

If you choose to export, back up, send, or share a file using your device or another app, that action is controlled by you and may be subject to the privacy practices of the app or service you use.

6. Children

Swibrary is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. Viori Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children through Swibrary.

7. Security

Swibrary is designed to keep diary-book data local to your device. Please protect your device with appropriate security settings, such as a screen lock and secure backups, because anyone with access to your device may be able to access local files.

8. Changes to This Policy

Viori Studio may update this Privacy Policy when Swibrary changes. If future versions add features that collect, transmit, or share data, this policy will be updated to explain those practices.
